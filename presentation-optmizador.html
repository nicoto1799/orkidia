Optimization Metrics Explained

1. Risk % (Risk Rate)

What it measures: Of the records that pass the filter, what percentage are risky?

Formula: (Risky records in filtered data) / (Total filtered records)

Example: If 100 customers pass your filter and 15 default â†’ Risk % = 15%

Business Goal: Minimize (lower is better)

2. Approval % (Survival Rate)

What it measures: What percentage of total records pass the filter?

Formula: (Records that pass filter) / (Total records)

Example: If you have 1000 customers and 600 pass the filter â†’ Approval % = 60%

Business Goal: Maximize (higher is better for growth)

3. Controlled % (Risk Eliminated)

What it measures: What percentage of risky records are filtered out?

Formula: 1 - (Risky records in filtered data) / (Total risky records)

Example: If there are 200 risky customers and you filter out 150 â†’ Controlled % = 75%

Business Goal: Maximize (higher is better for risk control)

The Optimization Challenge

These three metrics are conflicting objectives:

  • Reduce risk â†’ Fewer approvals (lower growth)
  • Increase approvals â†’ Higher risk
  • Control more risk â†’ Fewer approvals

POL_OPTIMIZATOR finds the Pareto-optimal solutions where you can't improve one metric without worsening another, giving you the best possible trade-offs to choose from.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What file formats are supported?
A: Currently, CSV files are supported. Excel support is planned for future releases.
Q: What's the maximum file size?
A: There's no hard limit. The chunked upload system handles files of any size.
Q: How long are results stored?
A: Results are stored indefinitely and can be recovered using your session ID.
Q: How many cases will be generated?
A: It depends on your configuration. Typically ranges from hundreds to hundreds of thousands. The system shows an estimate before you start.
Q: Can I customize the optimization objectives?
A: Currently, the system optimizes Risk %, Approval %, and Controlled %. Custom objectives are planned for v2.1.
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Sessions are isolated, and you control data retention.
Q: Can I integrate this with my existing systems?
A: Yes, an API is available for programmatic access. Contact us for API documentation.
